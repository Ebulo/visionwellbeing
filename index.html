Third-party competent person

Inspection support aligned to the Factories Act, 1948.

Vision Wellbeing supports third-party inspection readiness, competent person engagement, verification documentation, and certification-ready reporting for industrial assets and high-risk operations.

Factories Act, 1948 Third-party inspections Competent person coordination Certification-ready records

Our role is to help sites prepare the equipment baseline, inspection scope, records, and closure workflow needed for third-party competent person review. This is especially useful where plants need stronger evidence for lifting equipment, pressure systems, and hazardous-process controls.

Scope clarity

Asset listing, inspection planning, statutory records, and site-readiness checks before review.

Execution support

Coordination for third-party inspections, observation closure, documentation upgrades, and follow-through.

Evidence handover

Cleaner reports, traceable action items, and inspection files prepared for internal and regulatory reference.
