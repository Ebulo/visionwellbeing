What the app does

A focused safety workflow for everyday driver behavior.

Live ride tracking

Drivers start a trip, complete a vehicle checklist, and track speed, distance, time, and route behavior from GPS data.

Speed and call alerts

Overspeeding and phone-call activity during rides are captured as safety events, helping supervisors identify risky patterns.

SOS escalation

The SOS screen can send an emergency signal and queue it for retry if the network is unstable, with a configured emergency phone number available inside the app.

Driver stats and rewards

Safe rides, unsafe rides, max speed, risk score, coins, reward claims, and reward history help turn safer behavior into a visible habit.

App screens

Real driver workflows, shown exactly where teams use them.

The app keeps the daily flow simple: start from the dashboard, ride with live speed feedback, use SOS when needed, claim rewards, and keep profile, vehicle, language, and road signage details in one place.
